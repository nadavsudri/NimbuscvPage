How it works

Point your camera.
Move your hands.

Nimbus uses MediaPipe hand tracking to read your gestures in real time. Each finger curl, hand height, and wrist rotation maps to a MIDI CC channel — fully configurable, zero calibration.

Camera to DAW in one hop.
