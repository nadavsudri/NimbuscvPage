Got Questions?

We've got answers.

Does this work with my DAW? +
Yes. Nimbus is VST3, AU, and AAX. Works with Ableton Live, Logic Pro, FL Studio, Reaper, Bitwig, Cubase, Pro Tools, and anything else that supports standard MIDI.
Can I really use it live on stage? +
Absolutely. Under 12ms end-to-end latency—well below the threshold of perception. A lot of users mount a small camera on a mic stand and perform with it like an instrument.
What hardware do I need? +
Your laptop's built-in webcam is enough. Or any USB camera. That's it. No gloves, no Leap Motion, no Kinect, no extra sensors. macOS 12+ or Windows 10+.
How much CPU does it use? +
8–15% of one core on a modern laptop. Runs on its own background thread, so audio stays bulletproof. You can perform with confidence.
Is there a money-back guarantee? +
30-day full refund if it doesn't work for you. We're confident you'll love it, but no risk.
Does my camera feed leave my machine? +
Absolutely not. Zero cloud, zero telemetry, zero tracking. The tracking core is open source—audit it yourself if you'd like.
Can I use it with my favorite synths? +
Yes. Standard MIDI Learn works with every synth. We include 40+ presets for Serum, Vital, Massive X, Diva, Pigments, Phase Plant, and more.
What if I need help? +
Email hello@nimbus.audio or join our Discord. We read everything and respond fast. You're not buying from a faceless corporation.
